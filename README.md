# BMW_immatriculations
# A project on BMW immatriculations built off 3 different parts: cross section, time series and classification.
In the cross section phase the aim is to research the best model to better understand which possible variables, within the selectioned, may have the most influence on the rise or fall of car immatriculated at BMW. In the time series parte the research is of the model which can predict best the total number of immatriculations at BMW in the years to come, to obetter understan the total number of sales that cdan be within expectations. In the classification part a comparison between BMW, Audi and mercedes has been made and using the historical data is made a prediction of the market leader, between these 3 car brand, regarding car immatriculations.
